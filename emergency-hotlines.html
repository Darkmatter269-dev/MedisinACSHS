Emergency Hotlines

Tap a hotline to open a dedicated page with guidance and contact options.

Medical Emergency

Call: 911
Use for: Severe breathing issues, unconsciousness, chest pain, severe bleeding.

Fire Emergency

Call: 911
Use for: Active fire, smoke in building, electrical fire, evacuation support.

Police Emergency

Call: 911
Use for: Immediate danger, violence, suspicious activity, urgent public safety threats.
